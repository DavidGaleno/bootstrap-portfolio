Camping To Be Stylish

How we helped Explore to appear more "cool" to clients

Camping is usually associated with getting dirty, eating not conventional food and doing things in a old way. With this in mind, Express, a big camping company was having a lot of financial trouble, due to young ones losing interesting on camping.That said, we decided to make partnership with brands like Nike, Gap and Calvin Klein to make new suits of camping. Also, all new ads and TV commercials would show camping in a stylish way of doing.Express increased it's profit on 1503%, a value that's expect to grow.
